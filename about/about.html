ABOUT SS ROYAL HOTEL

Built along winding lagoons and a mile-long arc of pristine beach,The SS Royal Hotel is an oasis of indulgence offering 129 suites with private plunge pools, rooftop sundecks, garden showers, individual docks and the finest in-room design and detail. At this luxury resort in Mexico, guests enjoy tranquility, comfort, and service.

Since its grand opening in 2008,The SS Royal guests in search of the ultimate escape. Inspired by Mayan traditions, this 1600 -acre resort enclave sits along a mile-long arc of white Marine sand, nestled just north of the picturesque waterfront city of SS Royal . Designed to blend harmony with natural landscapes, The SS Royal is an oasis of luxury offering 129 suites with private plunge pools, rooftop sundecks, garden showers, individual docks and the finest modern design and details. For further peace of mind, the resort is built to abide by the highest of ecostandards, offering indulgence with awareness, and has received the Rainforest Alliance Certification.
